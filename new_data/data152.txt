yeh kya ban gaye hain hum
yeh kaise zindagi barbaad kar li hai
kaisi khushi ke peeche parre hain
jo kabhi mujhe mil hi nahi sakti
socha tha tumein apna keh payeinge
lekin yeh bhi kya khwaab dekha tha

ab koi ichha bhi nahi rahi
ab to koi armaan nahi raha

sochte hain ke kab yeh zindagi ki saza khatam hogi
kab is dil chain milega

tumein door jaate huye dekhte, mera kya haal hai
tumein khote huye, kya beet rahi hai

lekin, ab to maan lena parrega
akele is duniya mein aye, akele raheinge, akele jayeinge

khuda kare, mujhe kabhi kissi bhi umr mein kisi se pyar na ho

dil ne uddne ki ichha ki ek parri ko dekh kar
 jab pankh hillaye, jaana ke yeh dil to ek pinjare mein
bandh hai
Rooh ne cheekh cheekh ke pukara apne pyar ko
 jab apni hi awaaz waapas aayi, jaana ke isse to ijazzat hi
nahi cheekhne ki

 jinse milaa mere dil ko chain
koi jaane na kyun mujhse sharmaayein
  kaise mujhe tadfaayein

 tu hi sune aur main gaataa jaaoon
tu jo rahe saath mere
  duniyaa ko Thukraaoon
teraa dil behlaaoon

  kaise koi apne dil ko bachaaye
paas hai tu phir bhi jaloon
  kaun tujhe samjhaaye
saavan beetaa hi jaaye

  kho ke tujhe milne ki raah na paaoon
aisaa na ho jab bhi teraa
 naam labon par laaoon
main aansoo ban jaaoon
