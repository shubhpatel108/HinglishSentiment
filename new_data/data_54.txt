"savere ke samay meri yah pahali-pahali bohani he chappalen to main ab vapas nahin doonga" manu lachar hokar usase kaha, "ye chappalen mahatma gandhi kee hain tum inhen lauta do bhaee" yah sunakar mochi garv se bhar utha bola, "tab to main bada bhagyavan hoon aisa mauka mujhe bar-bar thode hi milane vala hai main bina majadoori liye hi bana doonga lekin chappalen lautaoonga nahin" kafi vad-vivad ke bad manu chappalen aur mochi donon ko lekar gandhiji ke pas pahunchi aur mochi donon ko lekar gandhiji ke pas pahunchi aur unhen sari kahani sunaee gandhiji ne mochi se kaha, " tumhen tumhara bhag hi to chahie n? tum mere guru bano main tumhara chela banata hoon mujhe sikhao ki chappalon kee marammat kaise kee jati hai?" aur gandhiji sachamuch us maile-kuchaile mochi ko guru bana kar chappal sine kee kala sikhane lage ve apane milane valon se baten bhi karate jate the aur sikhate bhi jate the guru-chele ka yah drishy dekhakar mulakati chakit rah gaye unhonne janana chaha ki bat kya hai? gandhiji bole,"yah ladakee mujhase bina poochhe meri chappalen marammat karane de aaee thi isalie isako mujhe sabak sikhana hai aur is mochi ko isaka bhag chahie, isalie mainne ise apana guru bana liya hai" fir hansate hue unhonne kaha, dekhate hain n aap mahatmaon ka maja!"

aisi safaee hamen rakhani chahie
